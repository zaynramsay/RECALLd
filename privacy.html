Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2024

Information We Collect

RECALLd is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect or store any personal information. The app functions by retrieving publicly available recall information from the FDA's API.

Use of FDA API

Our app interacts with the FDA's public API to retrieve recall information. When making requests to this API, only the information necessary for the request (such as search parameters) is sent. No personal user data is transmitted to the FDA through this process.

Data Storage

Any data stored on your device (such as your preferences or search history) is kept locally and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
